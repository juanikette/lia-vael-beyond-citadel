# Constraints

## Hard Constraints (Do NOT)

- Do NOT add romance with Lia
- Do NOT override Tali's role or importance
- Do NOT create mandatory interactions
- Do NOT break mission triggers or progression
- Do NOT assume Tali is always present unless handled conditionally

## Design Constraints

- Respect quarian pilgrimage rules
- Lia does NOT instantly complete her pilgrimage
- Normandy work may contribute, but does not fully resolve her arc
- Prefer additive changes over replacements
- Do not override base-game content unnecessarily
- Do not break existing missions or dialogue flows

## Implementation Guardrails

- Keep modifications isolated and modular
- Prefer safe, reversible changes
- Validate dialogue triggers and mission-state conditions carefully
- Avoid touching unrelated systems
