# Design Principles

## 1. Lore Consistency First

- Respect quarian pilgrimage rules.
- Lia does NOT instantly complete her pilgrimage.
- Normandy work may contribute, but does not resolve her arc fully.

## 2. Tali as Narrative Anchor

- Lia never replaces or competes with Tali.
- Tali functions as:
  - mentor
  - bridge to the Normandy
  - narrative justification for recruitment

## 3. Secondary Character Philosophy

Lia should be:

- present
- evolving
- emotionally meaningful

But not:

- critical to main plot progression
- required for gameplay success

## 4. Minimal Intrusion Design

- Do not break existing missions or dialogue flows.
- Do not override base-game content unnecessarily.
- Prefer additive changes over replacements.
